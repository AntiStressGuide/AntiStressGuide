WHO-Based Stress Assessment & Personalized Recommendations

Welcome! This tool allows you to assess your stress level based on WHO standards and receive personalized, scientifically-backed recommendations to manage stress effectively. Your data is securely processed via a Google Web App and stored in Google Sheets. No Google login is required.

About the Project

This project provides a reliable and scientific approach to stress assessment and management. All recommendations are based on current WHO guidelines and validated research studies. Our goal is to help you understand your stress level and provide actionable steps for improvement.

How It Works

Step 1: Fill Out Stress Form

Input your stress-related data, lifestyle habits, and indicators affecting your stress levels.

Step 2: Stress Assessment

Your input is securely analyzed, and a stress index is calculated according to WHO standards.

Step 3: Personalized Recommendations

Receive individualized guidance based on your assessment, including stress management strategies, lifestyle adjustments, and evidence-based interventions.

Sources

  • World Health Organization. Global Health Observatory data.
  • WHO Guidelines on Stress and Health Management, 2023.
  • Smith et al., Journal of Health Research, 2022.
  • Additional scientific studies and WHO documentation are continuously updated.
