WHO Guidelines on Stress Management

The World Health Organization (WHO) has developed numerous guidelines on stress management over the past years. These guidelines provide evidence-based recommendations for assessing, preventing, and reducing stress, targeting both individuals and organizations. They reflect the latest research on mental health, workplace wellbeing, and lifestyle factors, ensuring that interventions are scientifically validated and globally applicable. Our project builds upon these WHO recommendations to deliver reliable, personalized stress assessments and guidance.
