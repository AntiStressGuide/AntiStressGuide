WHO-Based Health Analysis Project

Welcome to our health analysis project. This tool allows you to capture your health data based on WHO standards and receive scientifically grounded recommendations. Data is securely processed via a Google Web App and stored in Google Sheets. No Google login is required to fill out the form.
